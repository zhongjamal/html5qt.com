The ALUR Slik Ultra-Portable Pod System presents a dynamic card-style open pod system, featuring a 400mAh integrated battery with 2mL maximum pod cartridges. Streamlined in a beautiful rectangular chassis, the ALUR Slik implements minimalistic lines and crafted for portability and travel-friendly.
