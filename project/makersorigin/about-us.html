We Take Makers to the World

  • Makers Origin is a member of the Sanpower Group, a multi-billion-dollar conglomerate.

    Makers Origin is an open hardware accelerator platform that nurtures makers and innovators.

    Our mission is to provide the platform, support, and access to hard-to-find resources—all within a one-stop shop—so makers and innovators can focus on their project and have a higher chance of success.

Our FDA-approved factory in Shenzhen, the Silicon Valley of hardware, is capable of supporting makers and innovators in product development, prototyping, competitive-price sourcing, manufacturing, and mass production.

Our retail and online assets, together with our wide distribution network in US, Europe and Asia provides makers and innovators with the possibility of selling to more than 10,000 stores as well as established e-commerce channels.
