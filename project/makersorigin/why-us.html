Our Distinctive Advantages over Other Hardware Accelerators

  • Vertically Integrated Factories

    IDT International Ltd. is our sister company based in Shenzhen, a consumer electronics manufacturer found in 1977. IDT has its own vertically integrated factory which is FDA-approved and has over 2000 staff. We will use IDT's factory to do prototyping and production for you so that you have more control and visibility on the progress, face lesser risk, and enjoy lower cost. Additionally, operating in Shenzhen for 40 years, IDT has developed a mature sourcing network helping lower the expense of material and ultimately making your product more competitive.

  • Industry-Proven R&D Power

    IDT is more than a manufacturer. It has been designing and developing its own lines of products over the years. Under the consumer brand Oregon Scientific, IDT sales upscale lifestyle electronics around the world. Seasoned developers and designers will work closely with you to realize your idea and refine the product.

  • Global Retail Network

    Another major hurdle for makers is distribution. You need big retailers to drive volume. But competition is fierce and shelf space is scarce. Contacting retailers, setting up meetings, and convincing them your product is marketable can be a difficult task. By working with us, makers can rest easy knowing their shipment will hit the shelves of established retailers in the world such as Brookstone (US specialty retailer with 300+ stores), House of Fraser (UK’s 3rd largest department store chain), Hamleys (world's most famous toy shop with 100+ stores), FunTalk (consumer tech retailer with 2000+ stores in China), and more.
