Go-To-Market Solution for Hardware Makers

Makers Camp APPLY

Duration: 90 days (Rotation)

The camp takes place in Shenzhen China, inside our campus where the factory and labs located. Here we will help bring your idea into prototype or improve your existing ones. We will provide you free workspace, tools, accommodation along with gym, snacks, and coffee.

What does the camp do?

1. Turn your idea into actual functional prototype
Work with our R&D team and product planner to fine-tune your product and get in-house rapid prototyping at our factory.
  • Product conception

    A new product needs to be novel and add additional value to what’s already on the market. Focusing on the problems you intended to solve, our product planner will help find out what products are available currently, what is missing and determine any other value points to improve yours.

  • Development environment setup

    We will identity the technology involved in your project, have a thorough understanding of these technologies, review the reference designs, purchase the development kits if any is missing in our lab and validate the technology before incorporating it into a product.

  • Design optimization

    From the aspects of hardware, firmware, mechanical, and software engineering, you and our R&D team will work out the best solution for the product.

  • Rapid prototyping

    We do all prototyping as well as product testing in-house, at our factory and laboratory so that you can always have full visibility and up-to-the-minute project progress. This also means lower cost and faster progress as there is no agencies and outsourcing involved. We will work closely with you to make ensure the cost stay within your budget.

2. Find you fund
  • Support your crowdfunding campaign
    1. You will need a compelling campaign page with good pictures and a video to communicate your innovative idea to crowdfunding supporters. Our photographers and graphic designers will give you a hand with it.
    2. To give you a head start, we will build anticipation and announce your campaign’s launch to our investor and retail partners.
    3. Our marketing experts will sort out a list of blogs and tastemakers with audiences that might be interested in your story and approach them on your behalf.
    4. To further engage with your supporters, we help you set up and run social media pages letting them know the latest progress you made.
  • Refine your pitch

    Luckily you are in Shenzhen, the Silicon Valley of hardware. Before we take you on the roadshow, you will get plenty of chances to practice pitching in the events of local hardware maker communities.

  • Maximize your startup roadshow

    We will help set up meetings with potential investors in China and the US and make air-tight travel plans that get the most out of your travel budget.

3. Equip you with real-world business knowledge
  • Lectures given by veterans in the industry

    We will invite industrial experts in hardware go-to-market business to our campus to teach new thinking, new knowledge on growing a startup into successful business.

  • Mentorship from seasoned product manager

    Our sister company IDT has four product lines in consumer electronics and several brilliant product mangers onboard. We will schedule weekly meeting for you to discuss with them about your process and refine the business model.

What will you get from this camp?

Ideally, an engineering prototype (looks good, works well) ready to be manufactured, sufficient fund, and business knowledge.

What is next?

Make hay while the sun shines. Our factory will start the manufacturing process. Sample units will be produced for testing. Once it is ready, we will start the mass production. Quality final products will be shipped to your eager backers on schedule.

Meanwhile the business plan will be finalized. Our sales team will start selecting retailers then push the shipment through our global distribution channel to the most suitable local retailers, both brick & mortar and online.

We will also help you in making marketing material such as product shots, key visuals, promotion videos, leaflets.

What kinds of applicants are we looking for?

Innovators of consumer or business-facing electronic devices, in the fields of (preferably but not limited to)

  • Health & Fitness
  • Education
  • Entertainment
  • Telecommunication
  • Lifestyle

With or without a prototype, if you think your idea is good enough, we would love to hear from you.
